03 · How it works

A simple path.

01

The Intake

A 30-minute consultation to map your current landscape and ensure our chemistry aligns. We focus on the concrete challenges of your return.

02

The Blueprint

We define 3 core pillars for your coaching journey. This is your personal roadmap—targeted, honest, and achievable.

03

Active Sessions

Bi-weekly 60-minute calls. We tackle real-time friction at work and home, refining your tools as you apply them.

04

The Sustain Phase

A final wrap-up session to cement your new boundaries and ensure you have a long-term support structure in place.
