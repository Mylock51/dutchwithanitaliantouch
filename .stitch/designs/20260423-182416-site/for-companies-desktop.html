01 · The problem

Return-to-work is the single biggest attrition risk for your high-performing female talent.

Between months 4 and 12 of returning from leave, many mothers feel the friction between their previous professional identity and their new reality. Without a bridge, they leave.

A program, not a benefit.

Structured 1:1 coaching sessions tailored to the executive context, focusing on role re-definition and sustainable performance.

Outcomes

Increased self-efficacy, reduced burnout risk, and a clear roadmap for the first 90 days back in the office.
